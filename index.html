Immutability 铁律
Once filed, a prediction's claim, date, resolution criterion and confidence never change. Amendments are new, dated entries in the corrections log; the original stands. The filing block is frozen by design.
Criteria at filing 立此存照
Every prediction states, at the moment it is filed, a resolves_by date and a one-line "resolves how" test. A claim with no falsification condition does not file.
Confidence + Brier 校准
Each call carries a probability. The Brier score is the mean squared error of those probabilities against outcomes — it punishes both cowardice (everything at 55%) and swagger (everything at 95%). Lower is better; a good record reads red, never green.
Dual grading 双栏评分
At resolution a call gets two marks: outcome (对 / 错) and mechanism (held / didn't). A prediction can be right for the wrong reason; the second column is where the learning lives.
Void 废
废 is reserved for a claim made genuinely ungradeable by events its criterion could not anticipate — never for a change of mind. Voids carry a dated reason and are excluded from the score.
